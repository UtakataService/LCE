# Organizational Use Requests

The bundled `LICENSE` grants broad rights to people acting in their own
individual capacity. It does not grant rights to Organizations.

For organizational use, contact the maintainer through
<https://utakataservice.com/contact/contact.php> with the subject
`Organization-use request`. Include:

- organization name and country/region;
- intended internal, research, product, hosted-service, or redistribution use;
- whether source changes or redistribution are planned;
- the Pack, model, tool, or deployment scope involved; and
- a technical and business contact.

No permission is granted until the copyright holder provides written approval.
Do not include credentials, personal data, or confidential customer data in a
public issue.
