# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, evidence-oriented collaboration space.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss ideas and evidence without attacking people.
- State uncertainty and limits clearly.
- Treat safety, privacy, licensing, and provenance concerns seriously.
- Welcome technically grounded disagreement.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, publication of private information,
deliberate misrepresentation of test evidence, or attempts to bypass security
and contribution boundaries are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation to protect people and
the project. A reporting contact will be added with the first public release.
